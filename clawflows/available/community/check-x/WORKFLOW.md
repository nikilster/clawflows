# X Engagement

Manage your X presence — notifications, engagement, and content.

## 1. Check Notifications

Using your X/Twitter skill or browser skill, scan for:
- Mentions and replies
- DMs from real people
- New followers
- Quote tweets

## 2. Categorize

- **Respond Now** — Direct questions, DMs, help requests
- **Engage Today** — New notable followers, nice comments, relevant conversations
- **Skip** — Bots, spam, hostile replies, engagement bait

## 3. Respond to Key Mentions

For high-priority mentions:
- Read the full thread for context
- Match the energy (casual for casual, professional for professional)
- Add value and be concise

## 4. Original Content (Optional)

If appropriate, suggest 1-3 posts: observations, lessons learned, progress updates, or helpful tips. Quality over quantity.

## 5. Privacy and Safety

- **NEVER share personal info** about the owner
- **NEVER delete posts** without permission
- **NEVER engage in damaging arguments**
- Stay in your lane — avoid unverified breaking news and politics unless that is the user's brand

## 6. Present Summary

Report:
- Notification counts (mentions, followers, DMs)
- What you responded to and why
- Notable new followers
- Engagement opportunities worth considering
- Posting suggestions based on what is resonating

## Notes

- Check 2-3x daily max. Batch engagement in 15-30 min sessions.
- Mute liberally, block rarely.
- It is okay not to respond to everything.
